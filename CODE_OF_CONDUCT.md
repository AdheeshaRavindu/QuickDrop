# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming, inclusive, and harassment-free community for everyone. We value the participation of each member and want all to have a positive experience.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contribute to creating a positive environment include:

- **Being Respectful**: Treat everyone with courtesy and respect
- **Being Inclusive**: Welcome people of all backgrounds, experiences, and perspectives
- **Being Constructive**: Provide helpful, actionable feedback
- **Being Considerate**: Think about how your words and actions affect others
- **Listening**: Engage in good-faith dialogue and listen to different viewpoints
- **Taking Responsibility**: Acknowledge mistakes and work to improve

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- **Harassment**: Insulting, demeaning, or discriminatory comments
- **Abuse**: Personal attacks, threats, or intimidation
- **Discrimination**: Based on race, ethnicity, gender, sexual orientation, disability, religion, or any other characteristic
- **Trolling**: Intentionally disruptive behavior
- **Exclusion**: Deliberately excluding people from conversations or activities
- **Privacy Violations**: Sharing others' private information without consent

## Reporting

If you witness harassment or unacceptable behavior:

1. **Document It**: Note what happened, when, and who was involved
2. **Report It**: Contact the maintainers at the project's primary contact email
3. **Provide Details**: Include as much information as you're comfortable sharing
4. **Stay Safe**: Never feel obligated to directly confront someone if you feel unsafe

All reports will be:

- Taken seriously
- Kept confidential (except as required by law)
- Investigated promptly
- Treated fairly

## Enforcement

Community leaders will review reports and take appropriate action, which may include:

- Requesting clarification or apology
- Temporary warning
- Permanent ban from project

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

- All project spaces (issues, PRs, discussions, emails)
- Community events and meetings
- Official project communications

## Acknowledgment

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org), version 2.0.

## Questions?

If you have questions about this Code of Conduct, please reach out to a maintainer.
