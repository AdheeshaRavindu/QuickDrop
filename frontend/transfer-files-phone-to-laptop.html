Best way to transfer files from phone to laptop

For fast local or internet transfer, open Receive mode on your laptop first, then use your phone to join and send files. QuickDrop handles both file and text transfer.

Common use cases

  • Move photos from phone to laptop
  • Share PDFs and notes for class work
  • Send screenshots and docs quickly

FAQ: transfer files phone to laptop

Can I transfer files from Android to laptop using browser?
Yes. Open QuickDrop in browser on both devices and use QR/link pairing.

Can I transfer files from iPhone to laptop?
Yes. QuickDrop works with modern mobile browsers and desktop browsers.
